**Hogwarts Legacy** is set to release in less than a day. Yet, it is already hitting [high numbers of players on Twitch and Steam](https://www.maddgamer.com/blog/hogwarts-legacy-hits-big-numbers-on-twitch-and-steam). Now PlayStation has announced the limited edition DualSense PS5 Controller for Hogwarts Legacy.

#### only on PlayStation Direct With Limited quantities

The limited edition controller will be available to pre-order the same day the game leaves early access on February 10th. The controller will be only available on the official [PlayStation Direct](https://direct.playstation.com/en-us/) in limited quantities. Sadly, you won't be able to obtain it by visiting your favorite retailers.

Gold-colored Ancient Magic design surrounds the controller with a beautiful black color. A simply beautiful controller if you are looking to add Hogwarts Legacy to your accessories. Once you purchase the controller it will be released on February 28th.
